Admin Module  
The Admin Module provides centralized control over the e-Sahayak platform by 
managing users, lawyers, legal documents, compliance, and system operations. 
It ensures secure, efficient, and regulation-compliant platform management. 
 
 
Lawyer Verification 
The system shall allow the administrator to verify advocate registrations by validating 
Bar Council enrollment numbers, identity documents, and professional credentials. 
The administrator shall approve, reject, activate, or suspend lawyer accounts. 
 
User Management 
The system shall enable the administrator to manage registered users by viewing user 
information, updating profiles, blocking or unblocking accounts, and resolving 
complaints. 
 
Document Template Management 
The system shall allow the administrator to create, update, categorize, and 
delete standardized legal document templates such as Rent Agreements, 
NDAs, Affidavits, and other legal forms. 
 
Compliance Monitoring 
The system shall ensure compliance with BCI regulations by reviewing lawyer 
profiles and removing unauthorized or promotional content. Only permitted 
professional information shall be displayed. 
 
Directory Management 
The system shall maintain a neutral lawyer directory organized alphabetically or 
geographically without rankings, reviews, ratings, sponsored listings, or AI-
based recommendations. 
 
Content Management 
The system shall enable the administrator to publish legal awareness articles, 
manage FAQs, upload government notifications, and moderate informational 
content. 
 
Platform Analytics 
The system shall generate reports on registered users, verified lawyers, 
generated legal documents, active subscriptions, and platform usage. 
 
Support and Complaint Management 
The system shall allow the administrator to manage support tickets, assign 

issues, monitor complaint status, and ensure timely resolution. 
 
Subscription and Payment Management 
The system shall manage software subscriptions, generate invoices, monitor payment 
records, and ensure no commission is charged on lawyer consultation fees. 
 
Audit and Activity Logging 
The system shall maintain audit logs for user logins, profile updates, document 
modifications, and administrative activities to improve security and accountability.
